Past Program Sites

Joel Spitzer has been developing and conducting smoking prevention programs since 1972. From 1978 to July of 2000 he ran smoking cessation clinics and school based smoking prevention programs for the Good Health Program of Rush North Shore Medical Center in Skokie, Illinois. Before joining the Good Health Program, he developed and conducted smoking education seminars for the American Cancer Society, Chicago Unit and Illinois Division. He has over 35 years of experience in the development and implementation of smoking cessation programs for adults and smoking prevention programs for children. He has conducted over 350 stop smoking clinics with over 4,500 participants. He has also presented over 690 educational seminars to approximately 100,000 children and adults on smoking prevention. Below are the last dates of programs at various sites throughout the metropolitan Chicagoland area.
