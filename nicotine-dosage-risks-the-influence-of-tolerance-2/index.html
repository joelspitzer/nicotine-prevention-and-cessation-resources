Nicotine Dosage Risks: The Influence of Tolerance

As people use nicotine over time, their bodies build up a higher tolerance. This means experienced users can take larger doses without feeling sick. Doses that longer-term users may claim to be safe because they themselves don’t feel sick from them, may be very dangerous for new nicotine users. This video aims to protect first-time and inexperienced nicotine users from the dangerous myth of nicotine tolerance – what may seem safe for long-term users may in fact be dangerous for more novice users.
